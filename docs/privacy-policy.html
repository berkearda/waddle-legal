Privacy Policy

Waddle - Focus Timer

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

Overview

Waddle - Focus Timer ("Waddle", "the App") is developed by Berke Arda. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

The short version: Waddle does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

Waddle does not collect any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no personal data fields in the App. All data generated through your use of the App — including timer statistics, fish balance, settings, and progression — is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Waddle uses the following third-party service:

  • RevenueCat — used to manage in-app purchases (Waddle Plus subscriptions and fish bundles). RevenueCat processes transaction data as part of Apple's standard In-App Purchase flow. No additional personal data is shared with RevenueCat beyond what Apple provides for transaction processing. You can review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for more details.

Waddle does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

In-App Purchases

Waddle offers the following in-app purchases:

  • Waddle Plus — auto-renewing subscriptions (weekly, monthly, or yearly) that unlock premium features. Subscriptions are managed entirely through the Apple App Store and can be cancelled anytime in your iOS Settings.
  • Fish Bundles — one-time consumable purchases that add fish currency to your balance.

All payment processing is handled by Apple. Waddle does not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Waddle does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Storage and Security

All App data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. No data is transmitted to external servers. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is removed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
