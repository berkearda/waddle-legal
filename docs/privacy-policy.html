Privacy Policy

Waddle - Focus Timer

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

Overview

Waddle - Focus Timer ("Waddle", "the App") is developed by Berke Arda. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

The short version: Waddle does not collect any personal information such as your name, email, or location. The App collects a small amount of anonymous usage data to power the leaderboard and improve the experience.

Data Stored on Your Device

Most data generated through your use of the App — including timer statistics, fish balance, settings, shop purchases, and progression — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is removed.

Anonymous Data We Collect

Waddle collects the following anonymous data, which is not linked to your identity:

  • Anonymous device identifier — a randomly generated ID stored on your device. This is not your Apple ID or any hardware identifier. It is used solely to distinguish entries on the leaderboard and associate anonymous analytics events.
  • Anonymous usage events — the App logs basic events such as session starts, completions, and abandonments, along with session duration. These events are used to understand how the App is used and improve the experience. No personal information is attached to these events.
  • Leaderboard data — if you complete focus sessions, your penguin name, total focus minutes, streak count, and fish balance are submitted to the leaderboard. This data is associated only with your anonymous device identifier.
  • User reports — if you report another user on the leaderboard, the report (including reporter and reported device IDs and the reason) is stored to help maintain a safe community.

This anonymous data is stored on servers provided by Supabase. No personal information (name, email, phone number, location, or Apple ID) is ever collected or stored.

Third-Party Services

Waddle uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — used to manage in-app purchases (Waddle Plus subscriptions and fish bundles). RevenueCat processes transaction data as part of Apple's standard In-App Purchase flow. No additional personal data is shared with RevenueCat beyond what Apple provides for transaction processing. You can review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for more details.
  • Supabase — used to store anonymous analytics events and leaderboard data as described above. You can review Supabase's Privacy Policy for more details.

Waddle does not use any advertising frameworks or third-party tracking technologies.

In-App Purchases

Waddle offers the following in-app purchases:

  • Waddle Plus — auto-renewing subscriptions (weekly, monthly, or yearly) that unlock premium features. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Subscriptions can be managed and cancelled in your iOS Settings.
  • Fish Bundles — one-time consumable purchases that add fish currency to your balance.

All payment processing is handled by Apple. Waddle does not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Waddle does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The anonymous data described above cannot be used to identify any individual. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

To delete all locally stored data, simply delete the App from your device. To request deletion of your anonymous leaderboard and analytics data from our servers, please contact us at nberkearda@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
